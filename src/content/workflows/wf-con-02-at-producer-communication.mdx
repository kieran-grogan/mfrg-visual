# At Producer: Communication

Sends automated carrier requests and SMS nudges while in the At Producer stage.

## Trigger
- **Added via workflow**: Typically from `wf-con-01-new-agent-enters-stage-no-profile`.

## Logic / Steps
1. **Submit Carrier Requests (Day 0 in S2)**: Automated email.
2. **Wait 1 Day**
3. **Submit Carrier Requests (Day 2 in S2 and Rotating)**: Automated email.
4. **SMS Nudge**: Friendly reminder via SMS.
5. **Wait (2) Days**
6. **Go To**: Loops back to step 3 for ongoing nudges until removed.
